Biography

Hello! I am currently working toward the M.Sc. degree in Control Science and Engineering with School of Automation Science and Electrical Engineering, Beihang University, directed by Prof. Xiwang Dong and Prof. Zhang Ren. My research interests focus on motion planning for mobile robots, such as aerial robots and unmanned ground vehicles.

In June 2021, I received the B.Eng. degree in Automatic Control with an outstanding graduate award from Beihang University, Beijing, China.
